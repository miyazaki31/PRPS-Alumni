# 🎓 PRPS Alumni

🌐 https://prps-alumni.vercel.app/

---

## 📌 About

**PRPS Alumni** is the official digital platform representing the alumni community of PRPS.
It serves as a central space where former students remain connected to their roots, their peers, and the institution.

The platform reflects the identity, achievements, and unity of the alumni network, highlighting the ongoing relationship between past and present members.

---

## 🌍 Purpose

The PRPS Alumni platform exists to:

* Strengthen lifelong connections among alumni
* Preserve shared memories and institutional history
* Highlight achievements and success stories
* Encourage collaboration, mentorship, and support within the community
* Maintain a bridge between alumni and the organization

---

## 🤝 Community

PRPS Alumni represents a diverse and growing network of individuals who have gone on to pursue different paths while staying connected through a shared foundation.

The community stands for:

* Unity
* Growth
* Contribution
* Legacy

---

## ✨ What This Site Represents

This website is not just a directory — it is a reflection of:

* The journey of PRPS members beyond their time in the organization
* The impact of the institution on its alumni
* A continuing story of connection and progress

## 📬 Stay Connected

Alumni are encouraged to remain engaged, support one another, and contribute to the ongoing growth of the PRPS community.
